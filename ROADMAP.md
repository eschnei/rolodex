# ROLO Roadmap

## Current Features
- Contact management with custom tags
- Cadence tracking and reminders
- Notes and action items per contact
- AI-powered note summarization
- CSV import with duplicate detection
- Dashboard with contact status overview

---

## Planned Features

### Gmail Integration
- **Email notifications** - Daily/weekly digest of contacts due for outreach
- **Auto-draft outreach** - AI-generated email drafts based on contact history and notes
- **Email tracking** - Automatically log when you email a contact
- **Thread detection** - Parse email threads to extract action items and update notes

### Smart Outreach
- **Outreach templates** - Customizable templates for different contact types
- **AI suggestions** - Context-aware message suggestions based on personal intel
- **Follow-up reminders** - Automated reminders if no response received
- **Best time to send** - Suggest optimal send times based on past engagement

### Contact Enrichment
- **LinkedIn sync** - Pull profile data and updates from LinkedIn
- **Company news** - Surface relevant news about contact's company
- **Mutual connections** - Show shared contacts and introductions
- **Social signals** - Track job changes, promotions, posts

### Analytics & Insights
- **Relationship health score** - Quantify relationship strength over time
- **Network visualization** - Graph view of your professional network
- **Outreach effectiveness** - Track response rates and engagement
- **Streak history** - Historical view of outreach consistency

### Collaboration
- **Shared contacts** - Team-based contact management
- **Warm intros** - Request and manage introduction requests
- **CRM sync** - Two-way sync with Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.

### Mobile
- **iOS app** - Native mobile experience
- **Quick log** - Fast contact logging after meetings
- **Widget** - Home screen widget for daily outreach
